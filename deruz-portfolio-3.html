The Person

Who Is
Deruz?

Dede Ruslan — known as Deruz — is a Brand & Marketing Strategist from Sukabumi, Indonesia, with over 6 years of hands-on experience building brand presence from the ground up.

Specializing in FMCG and community-driven campaigns, he led regional marketing operations at PT Nojorono Tobacco International (Clas Mild), managing teams and executing 50+ integrated campaigns that moved real market share.

Beyond corporate work, he founded Sahabat Lingkungan (saling.id) — an environmental organization that now reaches 100,000+ people across Sukabumi. That's not just marketing. That's leadership through storytelling.
